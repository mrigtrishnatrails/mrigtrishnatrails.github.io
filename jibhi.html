Overview

This is not a trip — it’s a slow journey into the untouched Himalayas. Designed for those who seek more than sightseeing, this experience blends culture, adventure, and deep relaxation.

Stay in luxury treehouses, explore ancient temples like Chehni Kothi, trek to hidden forts, and experience the untouched beauty of the Great Himalayan National Park.

Whether you're planning a corporate retreat, creative escape, or personal recharge — this is where you rediscover yourself.

Itinerary

Check-in → Riverside lunch → Leisure evening.
Hike to ancient wooden temple tower (1500 yrs old).
Trek to fort ruins → 360° Himalayan views → Picnic lunch.
Visit GHNP → Trout fishing → Forest walk.
Explore Bahu village → Café hopping → Work-from-mountains vibe.
Slow breakfast → Local market → Return journey.
