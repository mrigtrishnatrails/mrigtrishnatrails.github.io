Overview

Tucked away in the serene folds of Himachal, Barot Valley is where time slows down and nature takes over. Crystal-clear rivers, pine forests, and untouched Himalayan charm make this destination a hidden paradise.

Whether you're looking for a quick weekend escape or a deeper mountain immersion, Barot offers everything — from trout fishing in the Uhl River to trekking into remote villages and witnessing colonial-era marvels.

This journey isn’t about rushing — it’s about breathing, reconnecting, and experiencing the Himalayas the way they were meant to be.

🌿 Plan A: The Barot Breather (2N/3D)

Arrival in Barot → Check-in to riverside stay → Visit Barot Dam → Trout Fish Farm → Evening bonfire with grilled trout.
Hike to Haulage Trolley → Trek to Kothi Kohr → Scenic viewpoints → Evening departure.
Early morning arrival in Delhi.

🏔️ Plan B: The Uhl River Expedition (5 Days)

Check-in → Riverside picnic → Acclimatization → Slow mountain evening.
Trek to Winched Camp → Explore British engineering → Dhauladhar views.
Drive to Badagran → Trek to Rajgundha → Stay in luxury glamping tents.
Paragliding from Billing → Land in Bir → Monasteries & cafes.
Explore tea gardens → Monastery visit → Return to Delhi.
