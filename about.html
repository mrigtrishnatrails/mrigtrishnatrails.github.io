Our Story

Mrigtrishna Trails was born from a simple idea — that travel should not just be about reaching places, but about discovering yourself along the way.

In a world driven by speed, we believe in slowing down. In a world full of noise, we believe in silence. Our journeys are crafted to help you reconnect — with nature, with people, and most importantly, with yourself.

What started as a passion for mountains and trails has now grown into a community of explorers who seek something deeper than just destinations.
