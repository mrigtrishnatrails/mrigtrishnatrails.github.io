Overview

Short on time but craving Spiti? This trip is your perfect high-altitude escape. Cross the legendary Atal Tunnel, conquer Kunzum Pass, and explore the highest inhabited villages in the world.

From ancient monasteries to surreal landscapes and camping under a million stars at Chandratal — this is raw Himalaya in its purest form.
