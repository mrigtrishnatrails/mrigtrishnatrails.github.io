Overview

The Kedarnath Trek is not just a journey — it is an experience that blends spirituality, adventure, and raw Himalayan beauty. Nestled at an altitude of 3,583 meters, Kedarnath is one of the most sacred shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva and part of the revered Char Dham Yatra.

Starting from Gaurikund, the trail winds through dense forests, cascading waterfalls, and breathtaking mountain landscapes. Every step you take brings you closer not just to the temple, but to a deeper connection within yourself.

Whether you come seeking adventure or divine peace, Kedarnath leaves you transformed.

Day-wise Itinerary

Drive through scenic mountain roads along Alaknanda river. Overnight stay in Guptkashi.
Begin your trek from Gaurikund (16 km). Walk through waterfalls, forests, and mountain paths.
Attend morning aarti, explore temple surroundings, and soak in spiritual energy.
Descend from Kedarnath and drive to Rudraprayag. Relax and unwind.
Drive back with memories of a lifetime.
