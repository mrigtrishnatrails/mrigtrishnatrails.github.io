Jibhi Weekend Escape

2 Nights | Nature | Waterfalls | Hidden Himalayas

Duration

2N / 3D

Location

Jibhi, Himachal

Departure

Delhi

Trip Type

Weekend Chill

Overview

Escape the chaos of Delhi and step into a world where time slows down. Welcome to Jibhi — a hidden Himalayan paradise of pine forests, wooden cottages, and crystal-clear rivers.

This weekend escape is designed for those who crave peace without sacrificing adventure. From waterfall trails to secret riverside spots like Mini Thailand, every moment feels like a discovery.

Perfect for friends, couples, and solo travelers — this trip is your reset button.

Itinerary

Overnight journey in premium tempo traveller.
Check-in to wooden cottages → Visit Jibhi Waterfall → Explore Mini Thailand → Bonfire night.
Drive to Jalori Pass → Trek to Serolsar Lake → Sunset at Shoja village.
Early departure → Reach Delhi by morning.
