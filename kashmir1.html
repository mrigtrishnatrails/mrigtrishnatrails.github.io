Overview

Rediscover Yourself Through Nature Healing

A Kashmir trip from Delhi is more than just a holiday—it’s a thoughtfully curated journey that blends scenic landscapes, cultural richness, and moments of pure serenity. The experience begins with an overnight departure from Delhi to Jammu, where the transition from city life to mountain calm gradually unfolds. From here, a beautiful drive leads you to Srinagar, the heart of Kashmir. Staying in a traditional houseboat on Dal Lake is one of the most unique highlights of the trip, offering a peaceful ambiance surrounded by mountains and reflections on still waters. An evening Shikara ride adds a magical touch, especially as the sun sets over the lake. The journey continues with a day excursion to Sonamarg, also known as the “Meadow of Gold.” Here, the beauty of Kashmir feels raw and untouched, with the Sindh River flowing alongside and the majestic Thajiwas Glacier offering a glimpse of snow even in warmer months. This is followed by a visit to Gulmarg, a destination famous for its sweeping meadows and the iconic gondola ride that takes you high above the clouds, revealing panoramic views of snow-covered peaks. Back in Srinagar, the journey slows down with visits to the Mughal gardens—Nishat Bagh and Shalimar Bagh—where symmetry, greenery, and history come together beautifully. A stop at Shankaracharya Temple offers a peaceful spiritual moment along with a breathtaking view of the valley below. The experience then shifts to Pahalgam, reached via the saffron fields of Pampore. In Pahalgam, the sound of the Lidder River and the surrounding pine forests create a deeply relaxing environment, ideal for unwinding. The final part of the journey includes visits to Aru Valley and Betaab Valley, both known for their untouched landscapes and cinematic beauty. As the trip concludes with a return to Jammu, what stays behind are not just photographs, but memories of quiet lakes, flowing rivers, and majestic mountains. This journey captures the true essence of Kashmir—offering a perfect balance of exploration, comfort, and soulful connection with nature.
